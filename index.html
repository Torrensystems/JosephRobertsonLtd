Who We Are

From Scotland’s Seafood Heritage to International Markets

Joseph Robertson brings together deep Scottish seafood heritage and modern production capability, supplying value-added products for retail and foodservice markets.

With roots dating back to 1878, the business has evolved through generations while maintaining a clear focus on quality, consistency and dependable service.

Rooted in Heritage

Built on Scotland’s long seafood tradition, with generations of industry knowledge behind the business and a strong foundation in quality, trust and continuity.

Built for Today’s Market

Modern production capability aligned with the demands of retail and foodservice, supporting products that combine presentation, consistency and commercial practicality.

Focused on Partnership

A dependable, long-term approach to supply, built around service, flexibility and relationships that support customers across the UK and international markets.
